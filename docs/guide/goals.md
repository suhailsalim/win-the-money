# Goals & gamification

## Goals ("quests")

A goal has a target amount, an optional deadline, and a monthly contribution estimate. Goals are
grouped as **Active**, **Paused**, and **Achieved**, with on-track status derived from your progress.

## Back a goal with real assets

Instead of updating "saved so far" by hand, allocate real assets to a goal:

- a **deposit** (FD/RD), an **investment**, a **bank account** — each at a percentage — or a fixed
  **cash** amount.

Progress then computes itself from live values: your emergency-fund goal grows when the FD accrues or
the fund NAV rises, with no manual updates. Allocate from the goal's menu ("Allocate asset") or from
any asset's context menu on the accounts screen ("Quick allocate").

## Milestones

A net-worth **milestone ladder** (₹1L, ₹5L, ₹10L, …) tracks your liquid net worth on the Wealth tab;
the active rung shows live progress.

## XP, levels & badges

A light, fully local motivation layer — no leaderboards, nothing shared:

- **XP & levels** — accrue from good money habits; levels are named
  (Saver → Builder → Investor → Strategist → Master).
- **Badges** — achievements like keeping a multi-month streak of staying under budget.
- **Streak** — consecutive months within your plan.

It's cosmetic recognition, computed from your real data.

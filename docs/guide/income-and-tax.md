# Income & tax

## Income streams

Model each source of income — salary, freelance, rent — with an annual or monthly figure, an optional
linked account, and the day it credits. Non-INR streams convert at live FX rates. Streams feed the
expected monthly inflow on Home and the tax estimate.

## Payslip import

Import a salary-slip PDF and the app extracts gross, components, and TDS, projecting your annual
income — so the tax estimate reflects reality, not just your declared CTC.

## The tax estimate

A real Indian slab engine (FY 2025-26), not a rule of thumb:

- Computes **both regimes** — old and new — and recommends the cheaper one.
- Handles the §87A rebate (including the ₹12L new-regime cliff), surcharge, and cess.
- Adapts to your **track**: salaried, self-employed / professional (44ADA presumptive), business
  (44AD), or mixed.

The estimated tax figure shown across the app is computed from this engine and your income streams —
it updates as your income does.

!!! warning "Estimate, not advice"
    Slabs are maintained as data in the app and updated yearly, but this is an estimate for planning —
    not tax advice or a filing tool.

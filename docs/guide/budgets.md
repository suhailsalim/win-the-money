# Budgets & the Plan tab

## Category caps

Each budget category has a cap for its **cycle**:

- **Monthly** (default) and **quarterly** — aligned to the calendar.
- **Annual** or **custom N months** — anchored to a date you pick (an insurance renewal, the
  financial-year start), so "₹40,000/year on insurance" tallies over the right window.

The monthly overview folds longer cycles in at their per-month equivalent, so the headline
"planned vs spent" bar stays honest.

## Period navigation

The Plan tab shows **Monthly** by default; switch to **YTD**, **FYTD** (April–March), **calendar
year**, or **financial year**, and page through periods with ‹ › . Past periods show what you actually
spent per category in that window.

!!! note
    Caps are currently global — a past month is compared against today's cap, not the cap you had
    then. (Historical cap snapshots are planned.)

## Drill-in

Tap a category's **view transactions** button to see exactly which transactions make up its figure —
same for any bank or card from the accounts screen.

## What counts as spending

Transfers (card bill payments, moving money between your own accounts) and refunds are recognised
automatically and excluded from category spend, so paying your credit-card bill doesn't double-count
the month.

## Live Activity

A monthly-budget Live Activity (Lock Screen / Dynamic Island) shows spent vs plan and days left in
the month.
